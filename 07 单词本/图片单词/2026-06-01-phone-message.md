## 1. 核心名词 Nouns

### 1. signal

- 音标：/ˈsɪɡnəl/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：信号；暗号；提示
- 常见用法：可指手机信号、交通信号，也可指某种提示。
- 常见搭配：a strong signal；a weak signal；traffic signal
- 造句：The signal at the top shows that the content can reach the receiver.

### 2. battery

- 音标：/ˈbætəri/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：电池；电量
- 常见用法：常用于手机、电脑、汽车等设备。
- 常见搭配：low battery；charge the battery；battery power
- 造句：The battery sign reminds us that the tool still has power.

### 3. goods

- 音标：/ɡʊdz/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：商品；货物
- 常见用法：通常用复数，指可以买卖或运输的物品。
- 常见搭配：buy goods；sell goods；deliver goods
- 造句：The Chinese words suggest that some goods have arrived.

### 4. content

- 音标：/ˈkɑːntent/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：内容
- 常见用法：指文章、信息、视频或通知中的具体内容。
- 常见搭配：the content of the text；main content；online content
- 造句：The content is short, but the meaning is clear.

### 5. data

- 音标：/ˈdeɪtə/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：数据；资料
- 常见用法：常用于信息、电脑、网络和统计语境。
- 常见搭配：collect data；important data；business data
- 造句：The words in the picture can be seen as simple data.

### 6. communication

- 音标：/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃn/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：交流；沟通；通信
- 常见用法：指人与人之间的信息传递。
- 常见搭配：daily communication；modern communication；communication skills
- 造句：This picture shows a common way of communication in daily life.

### 7. emergency

- 音标：/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənsi/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：紧急情况
- 常见用法：用于突发、需要快速处理的情况。
- 常见搭配：in an emergency；emergency call；emergency help
- 造句：The words make the situation look like a small emergency.

## 2. 核心动词 Verbs

### 1. request

- 音标：/rɪˈkwest/
- 词性：v.; n.
- 意思：请求；要求
- 常见用法：比 ask 更正式，可用于工作、通知和书面表达。
- 常见搭配：request sb. to do sth.；make a request；at one's request
- 造句：The short content requests the receiver to come quickly.

### 2. warn

- 音标：/wɔːrn/
- 词性：v.
- 意思：警告；提醒
- 常见用法：提醒别人注意危险、问题或紧急情况。
- 常见搭配：warn sb. about sth.；warn sb. not to do sth.
- 造句：The urgent words warn the receiver that he should not be late.

### 3. remind

- 音标：/rɪˈmaɪnd/
- 词性：v.
- 意思：提醒；使想起
- 常见用法：remind sb. to do sth. 提醒某人做某事。
- 常见搭配：remind sb. of sth.；remind sb. to do sth.
- 造句：The content reminds the receiver to deal with the goods soon.

### 4. appear

- 音标：/əˈpɪr/
- 词性：v.
- 意思：出现；似乎
- 常见用法：可指东西出现，也可表示“看起来似乎”。
- 常见搭配：appear in the picture；appear to be；suddenly appear
- 造句：Several Chinese words appear in the middle of the picture.

### 5. confirm

- 音标：/kənˈfɜːrm/
- 词性：v.
- 意思：确认；证实
- 常见用法：确认信息、时间、计划或事实。
- 常见搭配：confirm the news；confirm the time；confirm an order
- 造句：The receiver may need to confirm whether the goods have really arrived.

### 6. delete

- 音标：/dɪˈliːt/
- 词性：v.
- 意思：删除
- 常见用法：删除信息、文件、数据等。
- 常见搭配：delete a file；delete data；delete content
- 造句：After reading the content, the receiver may choose to delete it.

### 7. view

- 音标：/vjuː/
- 词性：v.; n.
- 意思：查看；看待；观点
- 常见用法：作动词时表示查看信息、图片或页面。
- 常见搭配：view the content；view a picture；in my view
- 造句：The receiver can view the content before making a decision.

## 3. 动词与地道短语 Verbs & Phrases

### 1. request sb. to do sth.

- 音标：/rɪˈkwest ˈsʌmbədi tə duː ˈsʌmθɪŋ/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：要求某人做某事
- 常见用法：较正式，常用于通知、工作安排、书面表达。
- 常见搭配：request him to come；request students to finish the task
- 造句：The content requests the receiver to come as soon as possible.

### 2. warn sb. about sth.

- 音标：/wɔːrn ˈsʌmbədi əˈbaʊt ˈsʌmθɪŋ/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：提醒/警告某人注意某事
- 常见用法：用于提醒别人注意问题或危险。
- 常见搭配：warn him about the danger；warn us about the change
- 造句：The short words warn him about an urgent business matter.

### 3. remind sb. to do sth.

- 音标：/rɪˈmaɪnd ˈsʌmbədi tə duː ˈsʌmθɪŋ/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：提醒某人做某事
- 常见用法：考试和写作中很常见，注意 sb. 后接 to do。
- 常见搭配：remind me to check；remind him to view the content
- 造句：The content reminds him to come and check the goods.

### 4. appear to be

- 音标：/əˈpɪr tə bi/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：似乎是；看起来是
- 常见用法：比直接说 is 更委婉。
- 常见搭配：appear to be urgent；appear to be important
- 造句：The content appears to be urgent because it is very short.

### 5. confirm the content

- 音标：/kənˈfɜːrm ðə ˈkɑːntent/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：确认内容
- 常见用法：用于确认看到的内容是否准确。
- 常见搭配：confirm the content；confirm the data；confirm the order
- 造句：Before leaving, he should confirm the content.

### 6. delete data

- 音标：/dɪˈliːt ˈdeɪtə/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：删除数据
- 常见用法：用于电脑、手机、文件等场景。
- 常见搭配：delete old data；delete personal data
- 造句：People should be careful before they delete important data.

### 7. view the content

- 音标：/vjuː ðə ˈkɑːntent/
- 词性：phrase
- 意思：查看内容
- 常见用法：用于阅读信息、页面、文件或图片。
- 常见搭配：view the content carefully；view online content
- 造句：The receiver needs to view the content carefully.

## 4. 英文图说示例 Description Practice

### 基础版

The picture shows an old communication tool with several Chinese words on it. At the top, we can see the signal and the battery sign. The content is short, but it is not useless. It tells the receiver that some goods may have arrived and requests him to come soon. The whole situation appears to be urgent.

### 提升版

In the picture, an old communication tool is showing a short piece of content. Although the content only has a few Chinese words, it gives a clear request: the receiver should come soon because the goods may have arrived. The signal and battery signs make the scene more realistic. From this picture, we can see that simple communication can still be useful when the content is direct and urgent.

### 可复用句型

- The picture shows ...
- At the top, we can see ...
- Although the content is short, ...
- It gives a clear request that ...
- The situation appears to be ...
- This reminds us that ...

## 5. 知识点与考核

### 知识点

- 语法点 1：Although 引导让步状语从句  
  Although the content is short, it gives a clear request.
- 语法点 2：宾语从句  
  The content tells the receiver that some goods may have arrived.
- 语法点 3：情态动词 may 表推测  
  The goods may have arrived.
- 语法点 4：request sb. to do sth.  
  The content requests him to come soon.
- 常见用法：remind sb. to do sth.，不要写成 remind sb. do sth.
- 易错点：goods 是复数意义，谓语常用复数，如 The goods have arrived.
- 可复用表达：The situation appears to be urgent.
