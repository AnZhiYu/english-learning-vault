## 今日新词讲解

### 1. bathroom

- 音标：/ˈbæθruːm/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：浴室；卫生间
- 常见用法：指家里或公共场所的洗手间、浴室。
- 常见搭配：go to the bathroom；clean the bathroom；a bathroom mirror
- 地道短语：go to the bathroom 上厕所
#### 翻译

1. 虽然这个卫生间很小，但它很干净，对客人来说很方便。
2. 用完之后，请把干净的毛巾放回卫生间。
3. 浴室镜子很清楚，所以我能看到身后的海报。
4. 如果卫生间有人用，你可以在院子里等几分钟。
5. 保持卫生间干燥可以减少污染并保护我们的健康。

#### 造句

1. Although the bathroom is small, it is clean and convenient for guests.
2. Please put the fresh towel back in the bathroom after you use it.
3. The bathroom mirror was so clear that I could see the poster behind me.
4. If the bathroom is occupied, you can wait in the courtyard for a few minutes.
5. Keeping the bathroom dry can reduce pollution and protect our health.

### 2. poster

- 音标：/ˈpoʊstər/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：海报；招贴
- 常见用法：用于广告、活动、电影、通知等。
- 常见搭配：put up a poster；a movie poster；a warning poster
- 地道短语：poster child 典型代表

#### 翻译

1. 学生们张贴了一张海报，提醒人们减少污染。
2. 这张海报看起来很简单，但内容很有用。
3. 一张警示海报被放在港口附近，因为天气很危险。
4. 例如，当商店有新鲜商品时，海报可以推广新产品。
5. 关于气候变化的海报让许多学生改变了态度。

#### 造句

1. The students put up a poster to remind people to reduce pollution.
2. This poster appears to be simple, but its content is useful.
3. A warning poster was placed near the harbor because the weather was dangerous.
4. For example, a poster can promote a new product when the shop has fresh goods.
5. The poster about climate change made many students change their attitude.

### 3. harbor

- 音标：/ˈhɑːrbər/
- 词性：n.; v.
- 意思：港口；心怀，窝藏
- 常见用法：作名词表示船停靠处；作动词表示心里藏着某种想法或感情。
- 常见搭配：a safe harbor；harbor doubts；harbor a dream
- 地道短语：harbor doubts 心存疑虑

#### 翻译

1. 许多船停在港口里，因为信号提醒它们有暴风雨。
2. 这个旧港口已经成了游客观赏日落的好地方。
3. 尽管他心存疑虑，离开前他还是确认了内容。
4. 来自不同国家的货物经常通过这个港口运输。
5. 事实上，港口不仅是船只停靠的地方，也是商业中心。

#### 造句

1. Many ships stayed in the harbor because the signal warned them about the storm.
2. The old harbor has become a good place for tourists to view the sunset.
3. Although he harbored doubts, he confirmed the content before leaving.
4. Goods from different countries are often delivered through this harbor.
5. In fact, the harbor is not only a place for ships but also a center for business.

### 4. courtyard

- 音标：/ˈkɔːrtjɑːrd/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：庭院；院子
- 常见用法：指被建筑围起来的开放空间。
- 常见搭配：in the courtyard；a quiet courtyard；a school courtyard
- 地道短语：courtyard house 庭院式住宅

#### 翻译

1. 一位歌手在院子里表演时，孩子们拍手鼓掌。
2. 院子里有一个小花园，它让房子看起来很安静。
3. 晚饭后，我的家人坐在院子里谈论日常生活。
4. 客人到家之前，院子被打扫干净了。
5. 如果你感到疲惫，可以在院子里走走，而不是待在室内。

#### 造句

1. The children clapped their hands while a singer performed in the courtyard.
2. There is a small garden in the courtyard, and it makes the house look peaceful.
3. After dinner, my family sat in the courtyard and talked about our daily life.
4. The courtyard was cleaned before the guests arrived at the house.
5. If you feel tired, you can walk around the courtyard instead of staying inside.

### 5. pollution

- 音标：/pəˈluːʃn/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：污染
- 常见用法：常用于环境、空气、水、噪音等话题。
- 常见搭配：air pollution；water pollution；reduce pollution
- 地道短语：noise pollution 噪音污染

#### 翻译

1. 空气污染正变得更严重，所以我们应该立刻采取行动。
2. 工厂必须减少污染，因为干净的水对每个人都很宝贵。
3. 这张海报要求人们不要把垃圾扔进河里。
4. 虽然污染是一个难题，但实际的习惯可以产生影响。
5. 例如，骑自行车可以帮助减少污染并促进健康。

#### 造句

1. Air pollution is becoming more serious, so we should take action at once.
2. Factories must reduce pollution because clean water is valuable to everyone.
3. The poster requests people not to throw rubbish into the river.
4. Although pollution is a difficult problem, practical habits can make a difference.
5. For example, riding a bike can help reduce pollution and promote health.

### 6. climate

- 音标：/ˈklaɪmət/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：气候；氛围
- 常见用法：既可指自然气候，也可指社会或工作氛围。
- 常见搭配：climate change；a warm climate；political climate
- 地道短语：climate change 气候变化

#### 翻译

1. 这座城市气候温和，所以许多人喜欢住在这里。
2. 气候变化可能影响农民能在市场出售的商品。
3. 如果气候变得太干燥，我们将需要更多水来浇花园。
4. 这篇文章解释了为什么气候对动物和人类都很重要。
5. 事实上，我们对气候问题的态度应该更严肃。

#### 造句

1. The climate in this city is mild, so many people enjoy living here.
2. Climate change may affect the goods that farmers can sell in the market.
3. If the climate becomes too dry, we will need more water for the garden.
4. The article explains why climate is important to both animals and humans.
5. In fact, our attitude toward climate problems should be more serious.

### 7. marriage

- 词性：n.
- 意思：婚姻；结婚
- 常见用法：表示婚姻关系或结婚这件事。
- 常见搭配：a happy marriage；marriage life；marriage proposal
- 地道短语：marriage proposal 求婚

#### 翻译

2. 虽然婚姻很重要，但年轻人应该做出实际的决定。
3. 她的父母要求她在结婚前认真思考。
4. 没有尊重的婚姻可能在日常生活中造成许多问题。
5. 在我看来，婚姻应该培养信任，而不是压力。

#### 造句

2. Although marriage is important, young people should make a practical decision.
3. Her parents requested her to think carefully before marriage.
4. A marriage without respect may cause many problems in daily life.
5. In my view, marriage should foster trust instead of pressure.

### 8. attitude

- 音标：/ˈætɪtuːd/
- 词性：n.
- 意思：态度；看法
- 常见用法：常接 toward/to 表示“对……的态度”。
- 常见搭配：a positive attitude；attitude toward life；change one's attitude
- 地道短语：attitude problem 态度问题

#### 翻译

1. 你对错误的态度会决定你进步得有多快。
2. 虽然考试很难，但他保持了积极的态度。
3. 实际的态度帮助我们冷静处理紧急问题。
4. 例如，当我的翻译有很多错误时，我不应该放弃。
5. 改变态度通常是习得新技能的第一步。

#### 造句

1. Your attitude toward mistakes can decide how fast you improve.
2. Although the test was difficult, he kept a positive attitude.
3. A practical attitude helps us deal with urgent problems calmly.
4. For example, I should not give up when my translation has many mistakes.
5. Changing your attitude is often the first step to acquiring a new skill.

### 9. clap

- 音标：/klæp/
- 词性：v.; n.
- 意思：鼓掌；拍手；掌声
- 常见用法：表示用手拍出声音，常用于表扬或欢迎。
- 常见搭配：clap one's hands；clap for sb.；give sb. a clap
- 地道短语：a clap of thunder 一声雷响

#### 翻译

2. 请等音乐停止后再鼓掌。

4. 当紧急消息出现时，没有人鼓掌或大笑。
5. 一声雷响让院子里的人跑进屋内。

#### 造句

1. The audience clapped loudly after the speaker finished his report.
2. Please do not clap until the music stops.
3. The children clapped for the winner because he showed great courage.
4. When the urgent news appeared, nobody clapped or laughed.
5. A clap of thunder made the people in the courtyard run inside.

5. In fact, abolishing a bad habit is harder than making a plan.

- 意思：立方的；三次方的
- 常见用法：常用于数学、体积单位。
- 常见搭配：cubic meter；cubic centimeter；a cubic equation
- 地道短语：cubic meter 立方米

#### 翻译

1. 这个箱子的体积是一立方米。
2. 一立方厘米比一立方米小得多。
3. 数学老师让我们计算这个容器的立方体积。
4. 虽然 cubic 听起来难，但这个概念在现实生活中很实用。
5. 例如，建筑工人买混凝土时使用立方米。

#### 造句

1. The box is one cubic meter in size.
2. A cubic centimeter is much smaller than a cubic meter.
3. The math teacher asked us to calculate the cubic volume of the container.
4. Although cubic sounds difficult, the idea is practical in real life.
5. For example, builders use cubic meters when they buy concrete.

### 19. addicted

- 音标：/əˈdɪktɪd/
- 词性：adj.
- 意思：上瘾的；入迷的
- 常见用法：常接 to，表示“对……上瘾/着迷”。
- 常见搭配：be addicted to games；be addicted to smoking；get addicted to
- 地道短语：be addicted to doing sth. 沉迷于做某事

#### 翻译

1. 一些青少年沉迷网络游戏，所以睡得很晚。
2. 他过去沉迷吸烟，但现在已经戒掉了。
3. 如果你沉迷短视频，学习可能会受到影响。
4. 我的朋友并不沉迷咖啡；他只是喜欢它淡淡的味道。
5. 事实上，对任何东西上瘾都可能变成严重问题。

#### 造句

1. Some teenagers are addicted to online games, so they sleep late.
2. He used to be addicted to smoking, but now he has given it up.
3. If you are addicted to short videos, your study may be affected.
4. My friend is not addicted to coffee; he just likes its mild taste.
5. In fact, being addicted to anything can become a serious problem.

### 20. urgent

- 音标：/ˈɜːrdʒənt/
- 词性：adj.
- 意思：紧急的；急迫的
- 常见用法：形容事情需要立刻处理。
- 常见搭配：an urgent matter；urgent need；urgent action
- 地道短语：urgent care 急诊护理；紧急医疗

#### 翻译

1. 这条紧急信息要求他尽快赶来。
2. 虽然事情很紧急，但她冷静地确认了内容。
3. 我们应该采取紧急行动来减少污染。
4. 如果你的电量很低，充电就成了紧急需求。
5. 商店几乎卖完了，所以订新货很紧急。

#### 造句

1. The urgent message requested him to come as soon as possible.
2. Although the matter was urgent, she confirmed the content calmly.
3. We should take urgent action to reduce pollution.
4. If your battery is low, charging it becomes an urgent need.
5. The shop is almost sold out, so ordering new goods is urgent.

## 今日知识点汇总

> 以下知识点来自今日 20 个新词的例句，已经合并去重。

- although 引导让步状语从句：Although the bathroom is small, it is clean and convenient.
- because 引导原因状语从句：Factories must reduce pollution because clean water is valuable.
- if 引导条件状语从句：If your battery is low, charging it becomes an urgent need.
- when / while / after / before 引导时间状语从句。
- so 表结果，for example / in fact 用于举例和强调。
- there be 句型：There is a small garden in the courtyard.
- so ... that ... 结果状语从句：The bathroom mirror was so clear that I could see the poster.
- not only ... but also ... 表递进并列。
- not ... until ... 表“直到……才”。
- the more ..., the faster ... 比较结构。
- 动名词作主语：Keeping the bathroom dry can reduce pollution.
- to do 作目的状语：The students put up a poster to remind people.
- 被动语态：A warning poster was placed near the harbor.
- 情态动词 may / should / must / can 表推测、建议、必要或能力。
- request sb. to do sth. / request sb. not to do sth.
- remind sb. to do sth. / warn sb. about sth.
- be addicted to doing sth.
- acquire knowledge / acquire a skill / acquire experience.
- promote health / promote a product / get promoted.
- goods 作复数意义，常接复数谓语：The goods have arrived.
